About Us

Our roots come from humble beginnings in our backyard garden in Portland. We began to grow and dry herbs, giving them to friends and family to make teas or simple syrups. As word spread outside our network about our fragrant and well preserved herbs, we received requests from strangers and even local bars to use our herbs for simple syrups. We've continued our same methods of growing organic herbs and air/sun drying them to maintain their quality. Check out our small selection of organic herbs hand picked by family and stored in air tight, amber containers. Whether you use our herbs as a simple syrup or herbal tea for your health, know you are buying local, organic, ethical products with Flur Apothecary.
